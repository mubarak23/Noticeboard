 instruction 
step one : download the project folder from github and unzip it

step two: after unzipping the folder, rename the folder to noticeboard, i.e just as the name of 
the old project in your xampp htdocs

step three : open noticceboard.sql in your preferred editor

step four : drop the noticeboard database in your phpmyadmin.

Step five : create a new database called noticeboard just like the database name you find in the 
noticeboard.sql

step six : import the noticeboard.sql, by clicking on the import top on the top menu bar of your
new created database.
	if all thing goes successfull, all database table and it's dump data will be imported.

step: seven : carry your new project that you rename to noticeboard to your htdocs,

the run it on your preferred browser.
